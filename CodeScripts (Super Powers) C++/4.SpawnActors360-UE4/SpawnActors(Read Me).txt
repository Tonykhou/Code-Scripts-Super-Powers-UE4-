SpawnActors 360:
will spawn any object of the  actor subclass you chose several times around a circle.  



Variables:
(Float)Tetha: angle between each object.
(Float) Radius: Size of the circle.
(Bool) Deviate Orientation: Wether to randomize their rotations or not.

Instructions(How To Add to your Project). 
•Open unreal engine 
•Create a new c++ actor class
•copy and paste the.h and.cpp file into the.h and.cpp file of the newly created actor. 
• Replace all mentions of “themeshspawnee” with the name of your actor.
• Replace "MESHMAKER_API" with "Your project/plugin name"_API
